Database Systems Project

Last semester, we were tasked with creating a database to store information for a topic of our choice. I decided to create mine
for a website called auctionexport.com. The site is used to purhcase cars from abroad and import them.
You can also put your own cars up for sale. As the domain suggests, there are auctions where users bid on cars. The car goes to the user
with the highest bid at the end of the session.
